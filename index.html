Welcome to Rolebotics Africa

IMPACT

Our foundation's purpose is to make a significant impact by aligning our goals with national and international development agendas, contributing to the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) by promoting quality education (SDG 4), fostering innovation (SDG 9), and reducing inequalities (SDG 10). We aim to accomplish this by:
  • Increasing Technological Literacy: Enhancing the understanding and interest in technology among Nigerian youth.
  • Reducing Educational Disparities: Bridging the gap between privileged and underserved communities in terms of access to quality technology education.
  • Fostering Innovation: Encouraging students to think creatively and develop innovative solutions that can address local and global challenges.
  • Building Future Leaders: Inspiring and preparing the next generation of Nigerian leaders in technology and engineering.

OUR APPROACH

  1. Training Volunteers: We provide training for volunteers, starting with university students in Nigeria, equipping them with the foundational knowledge and skills in robotics. These trained volunteers become ambassadors, spreading their knowledge to secondary schools and underserved communities.
  2. Hands-On Experience: By providing access to advanced robotic tools, we enable students to see, touch, and interact with technology, turning theoretical knowledge into practical experience. This hands-on approach is crucial in sparking interest and understanding key aspects of modern technology and innovation.
  3. Community Engagement: We work closely with local communities to identify schools and groups that would benefit most from our programs. Our goal is to reach those traditionally left out of the technological revolution.
  4. Capacity Building: Our work aims to build broader technological capacity beyond robotics. By inspiring interest in relevant fields that are active players in the evolution of Industry 5.0, we contribute to creating a workforce better prepared for the challenges of the 21st century.
